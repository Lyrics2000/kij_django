Kijabe Printing Press
A Christian printing firm

Africa Inland Church (A.I.C) Kijabe Printing Press is a Christian based Private Company, which was established over 50 years ago. Initially it was established to print Christian literature, but later ventured into commercial printing. It’s one of the leading printing companies in Kenya and a growing brand in Africa.

Through the many years of experience in printing, A.I.C Kijabe Printing Press has cut for itself a niche in the market. We specialize in translating our clients’ ideas into high quality printed materials.

    Prepress

    This department originates all production work. Our highly qualified designers will give your manuscript the professional touch needed to make it attractive to your intended readers. Our modern computers loaded with hi-tech design programs, together with our state –of –the-art prepress machinery make us really efficient in what we do.

    Press

    This is where the actual printing takes place. We have at our disposal modern printing machines, which run at high speed while ensuring quality.

    BINDERY/BOOK FINISHING

    This department is equipped with highly efficient machines for both sandle-stitched and perfect bound books. Our UV vanishing machines for both full and spot vanishing give your cover the glossy appearance that makes it really attractive. Our state-of-the-art equipment gives you a wide range of finish effects.

    QUALITY ASSURANCE

    To guarantee quality, we have developed our operation system such that it tracks the job at every stage, right from origination to completion, with scrupulous attention to every detail to ensure that each copy produced is perfect.
